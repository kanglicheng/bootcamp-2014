KTH Boot Camp 2014, Möja, June 6 – 8

Venue and what to bring

STF Möja. We have the hostel 2 days for ourselves. In the evenings we can play card games, play the guitar, etc. Leave your computers at home!

Please bring your own bed sheets or a sleeping bag and a towel, they are not included in the price. Don't forget to bring a swimsuit as well. The hostel doesn't have a shower, but we can shower 5 minutes from the hostel at Möjahallen.

We will provide paper and pens for the offline competition, but maybe you want to bring your lucky pen, so don't forget it!

More information

At Möja we will have 2 offline competitions, where you will be solving problems on paper. Proofs will be as important as correct code.

We plan to have 3 or 4 lectures on Friday and Saturday in the evening. The topics aren't decided yet and we are open to suggestions – see below.

Saturday afternoon will be free and you can cycle, hike, swim or paddle. You can rent a bike or a kayak at Möja but you will have to pay for it yourself. A bike should cost around 100 crowns and a kayak 200–300 crowns. More information.
